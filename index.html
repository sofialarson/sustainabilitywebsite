SUSTAINABILITY


What is sustainability?

Sustainability is a goal. The aim is to maintain conditions where humans and nature can coexist to support present and future generations. Currently, we are falling short of that goal. At the rate that we are expending resources, the earth will not be able to support us for much longer.


Are YOU living sustainability?

Sorry to be the bearer of bad news, but the answer is likely no. It is for me too. We tend to think we do not produce THAT much waste, or waste THAT much water. But the only reason we have been living so comfortably without noticing our effect on the earth is because others do not have the same resources as us. If everyone lived as we do, the earth could not support us. Want to see exaclty how many earths we would need to support everyone if they lived your lifestyle? Try this quiz!

If everyone had the lifestyle of an average American, we would need about five earths to support them.
