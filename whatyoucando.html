HOW YOU CAN HELP

Where do you stand?

Think for a moment. How could you be more sustainable? What ways are you already practicing sustainability? You can take the global footprint quiz as a guide for these questions.

Recycling

Recycling is one of the easiest steps to first take towards sustainability. Just make sure to put the right waste in the right bin!

Food Habits

It takes more resources to produce meat than it does for plant-based food, so going vegetarian is great for the environment! Additionally, try to cut down on food waste. Use extra food for leftovers or donate it!

Conserving Resources

Conserving natural resources are pretty easy! Start off small, take less time in the shower, turn off your lights when you leave the room, etc. Then maybe step it up, maybe start a carpool to work.

Group up

The majority of harmful emissions come from corporations. The only way to combat that is through collboration. Join protests and sign petitions to enforce sustainability measures onto corporations!
